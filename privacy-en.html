1. Introduction

HTS ("we", "us", or "Developer") values your privacy when you use the Numly application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights when using the App.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Definitions

  • Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • Device Identifier: Advertising identifiers such as Apple's IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) or Google's GAID (Google Advertising ID).
  • Third-Party Services: Services used within the App but not controlled by the Developer (e.g., advertising networks).

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information We Do Not Collect

Numly does not collect the following from its users:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Date of birth, age, or gender
  • Address or location data
  • Payment information or credit card details
  • Social media account information
  • Photo, camera, microphone, or contact list access

The App does not require account creation. You can play offline without an internet connection.

3.2 Information Stored on Your Device (Not Our Servers)

For your game progress to work locally, the following data is stored only on your device:

  • Levels completed and stars earned
  • Total combo count and statistics
  • Language, theme, and color preferences
  • Daily reward claim date

This data is not sent to our servers. When you delete the App, this data is also deleted.

3.3 Automatically Collected Information (by Advertising Networks)

The App contains rewarded video ads served by Google AdMob. Ads are shown only based on your choice (extra moves, hints, daily reward). AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA/GAID) — A device-specific, resettable identifier
  • IP Address — For approximate location
  • Device Information — Operating system, device model, language setting
  • Ad Interaction — Which ads you saw, clicked, or skipped

This information is subject to AdMob's Privacy Policy.

4. How Information Is Used

Collected information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Core App functionality: Storing your game progress on your device
  • Ad delivery: Serving rewarded video ads through AdMob
  • App improvement: Understanding general usage trends from aggregate (anonymous) data provided by ad networks

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google AdMob

The App uses Google AdMob's advertising network. AdMob may use your device identifier to serve personalized ads.

  • Manage ad preferences: Google Ads Settings
  • iOS tracking permission: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Reset advertising ID on Android: Settings → Google → Ads

5.2 Apple App Store / Google Play

The App is downloaded and updated through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. These platforms have their own privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

Numly is intended for a general audience and is not specifically designed for children under 13. In compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

If parents believe their children's information has been collected inadvertently, they may contact us at htariksavas@gmail.com to request deletion.

The App can be configured to use Google's "Designed for Families" ad settings.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your country, you may have the following rights:

7.1 Rights under GDPR (European Union)

  • Right of access: Request access to data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): Request deletion of your data
  • Right to object: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Data portability: Transfer your data to another service

Note: Since Numly does not store data on its servers, most requests can be fulfilled by using your device settings (e.g., deleting the app or resetting your device's advertising ID).

7.2 Rights under CCPA (California)

California residents have the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. Numly does not sell personal information.

7.3 Rights under KVKK (Turkey)

To exercise your rights under Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data, please contact htariksavas@gmail.com.

8. Data Security

  • Game data stored on your device is protected by your operating system's standard app data protection
  • Since we do not send data to our servers, there is no server-side data breach risk
  • Third-party advertising networks implement their own security measures

9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The App uses your device's local storage (localStorage) to save your game progress and preferences. This is not a traditional web cookie and does not leave your device.

Advertising networks (AdMob) may use their own identifiers — see Section 5.1 above.

10. Data Retention

  • Device data: Until you delete the App
  • Ad data: Subject to AdMob's own retention policy (typically 24 months)

11. International Data Transfer

Our advertising networks (Google) may transfer data to the US or other countries. These transfers are secured through standard contractual clauses or other legal mechanisms.

12. Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will display a notification within the App. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
